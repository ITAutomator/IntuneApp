Adobe Reader DC (Free version)

How to update

1. Go to http://get.adobe.com/reader/enterprise
Enter these specs and download: Win 10, All Langs (MUI), Adobe Reader 64 bit

2. Put AcroRdrDC2*MUI.exe in the Setup folder
Delete any old ones
